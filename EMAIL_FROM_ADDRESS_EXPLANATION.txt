WHY YOU CAN'T USE staffos@scalingtheory.com DIRECTLY
================================================================================

RESEND'S REQUIREMENT:
Resend requires you to VERIFY the domain before you can send emails FROM that domain.

This is an email security requirement (SPF, DKIM, DMARC) to prevent spam.

WHAT THIS MEANS:
- You can ONLY send emails FROM addresses that use a VERIFIED domain
- Since you verified "staffos.io", you can use: staffos@staffos.io
- You CANNOT use: staffos@scalingtheory.com (unless you verify scalingtheory.com too)

WHY THIS LIMITATION EXISTS:
Email providers (Gmail, Outlook, etc.) check if the sending domain is verified.
If you try to send FROM an unverified domain, emails will:
- Be rejected by Resend (403 error)
- Or go to spam folder
- Or be marked as suspicious

ABOUT YOUR GOOGLE ACCOUNT (staffos@scalingtheory.com)
================================================================================

Your Google account staffos@scalingtheory.com is a RECEIVING email address.
It's not the same as a SENDING email address through Resend.

Resend is an email SENDING service (like SendGrid, Mailgun).
It sends emails ON BEHALF OF your application.

You can:
- Use staffos@staffos.io to SEND emails (via Resend)
- Use staffos@scalingtheory.com to RECEIVE emails (your Google account)

They serve different purposes:
- SENDING: staffos@staffos.io (via Resend) - sends welcome emails to employees
- RECEIVING: staffos@scalingtheory.com (Google) - receives emails sent to you

You can still use your Google account for:
- Receiving support emails
- Managing your business email
- Personal communication

But for sending automated emails (welcome emails, notifications), you need
a verified sending domain (staffos.io) through Resend.
